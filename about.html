Our Heritage

A Passion for Perfection

AquaElite was established in 2011 with a simple, singular goal: to elevate the standards of swimming pool maintenance in luxury coastal residences. We recognized that standard commercial cleaners lacked the chemical precision and attention to detail that luxury homeowners demand.

Today, our expert crews handle over a thousand residential and commercial properties, offering customizable digital health logs, environmentally safe water purifiers, and structural engineering compliance certifications.
