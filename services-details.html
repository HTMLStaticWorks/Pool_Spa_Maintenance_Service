Clinical-Grade Water Sanitization

Standard pool cleaning relies heavily on dumping high volumes of liquid chlorine directly into the pool. This results in red eyes, skin irritation, damaged swimwear, and strong bleach odors. AquaElite offers an alternative premium solution by integrating smart water sanitizers.

We calibrate ozone reactors, high-intensity ultraviolet (UV) sterilizers, and mineral release cartridges. Ozone gas and UV beams instantly destroy organic pathogens, viruses, and algae spores as they flow through the equipment room. This reduces standard chlorine usage by up to 70%, resulting in soft, silk-like water that is safe for sensitive skin.

Specialized Technology We Support

To achieve medical-grade water purity, we install, test, and calibrate premium systems from the industry's leading brands:

UV Sterilizers

Destroys chloramines and active protozoa that resist standard chemical sanitizers.

Ozone Generators

Produces active O3 gas inside a sealed chamber, oxidizing contaminants instantly.

IntelliChem Controllers

Computes precise chemical dosages based on live ORP (oxidation-reduction potential) values.

Mineral Sanitizers

Releases silver and copper ions into the water stream, naturally suppressing algae growth.

Sanitization Benefits

  • Hypoallergenic Swim: No red eyes, itchy skin, or chlorine odors.
  • Plaster Longevity: Non-corrosive chemical balance prevents tile erosion.
  • Automation Compliance: Handled entirely by intelligent chemical feeders.
  • Eco-Conscious Choice: Minimizes synthetic chemical discharge into public water networks.
