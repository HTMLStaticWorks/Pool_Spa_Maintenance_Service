Pricing & Service FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our billing methods, chemical inclusions, and policies.

Are chemicals included in the monthly package cost?

Yes, all basic balancing chemicals (liquid chlorine, acid, sodium bicarbonate, and soda ash) are included in all of our monthly service packages. Specialized chemicals like phosphate removers, water clarifiers, or algaecide treatments are billed separately as needed.

What happens if my scheduled service falls on a rainy day?

In the event of heavy rain or storms, our technicians still perform chemical tests, adjust sanitizer levels, and empty all filter baskets. For safety reasons, manual vacuuming and brushing will be postponed until your next scheduled visit or a clearer day.

Is there a long-term contract commitment?

No, all of our residential and commercial packages operate on a flexible month-to-month subscription model. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your service package at any time with a 30-day notice.

Do you offer specialized support for salt-water chlorinators?

Absolutely. We specialize in salt water swimming pools. Our technicians regularly clean salt cells, check salt density levels in the water, and test salt generator electrical connections to keep your sanitation system running smoothly.
