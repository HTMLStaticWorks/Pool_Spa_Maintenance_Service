Pure Water Science

Water Balancing Technology

Pure water is not just about clear colors; it's about biochemical wellness. If water is acidic, it damages metal heater components. If it's too basic, it creates scaling layers across the pool lining.

Our water calibration protocols examine 7 distinct metrics: Free Chlorine, Combined Chlorine, pH levels, Total Alkalinity, Calcium Hardness, Cyanuric Acid stabilizer, and Total Dissolved Solids (TDS).
